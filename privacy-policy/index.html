Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Screenshot Deleter handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app.

Photo Library Access

Screenshot Deleter requires access to your photo library to identify and help you delete screenshots. All photo processing happens directly on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos to any external servers.

Information Collection and Use

Our app collects anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the app experience. This data cannot be used to identify individual users and includes basic information such as app launches and feature usage.

Data Storage

Any app preferences or settings are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any user accounts or store personal information on external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
